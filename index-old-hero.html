The Artist

Boston artist Stephen Sironi paints life as he sees it. His work captures snapshots of everyday life — the beautiful complexity of urban existence where old buildings stand beside new, where people mingle yet remain solitary, where light plays against dark.

The world is not simple; it's very complicated. Beautiful, dirty, contrasting, invigorating, confrontational, and sometimes underwhelming in its predictability. Steve's paintings explore this rich tapestry of life.

Born and raised just south of Boston, Steve came of age in an artistic era of experimentation, discovery, and turmoil. With a degree in fine arts from UMass Amherst, Steve has lived a life full of creativity and artistic expression. He currently resides in central Ohio.

— Stephen Sironi
